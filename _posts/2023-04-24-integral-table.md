$$
\int A x^2 \sin^2(kx) \, dx = \frac{A}{6} x^3 - \frac{A}{4k^2} x^2 \sin(2kx) - \frac{A}{4k^3} x \cos(2kx) + \frac{A}{8k^3} \sin(2kx) + C.
$$
