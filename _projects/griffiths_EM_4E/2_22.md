## Part A

We calculated the electric field for this configuration in <a href="https://coltonkawamura.github.io/coltonkawamura/projects/griffiths_EM_4E/2_13/">Problem 2.13</a>. Usually, we'd walk the line integral from $$s=\infty$$ to the point $$s$$. But since the charge configuration is infinitely long, 

$$
\begin{align*}
V_\text{}&= -\int_\infty^{s'} \mathbf{E}\cdot d \mathbf{s} \\
&= -\int_\infty^{r'}  \left( \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0}\frac{q}{r^2} \right)\hat{r} \cdot dr \hat{r} \\
&=\left.\frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} Q \left( \frac{1}{r} \right)\right|_{\infty}^{r'} \\
&= {\frac{q}{4\pi\epsilon_{0}}}{\frac{1}{r}}.\label{1} \tag{1}
\end{align*}
$$
