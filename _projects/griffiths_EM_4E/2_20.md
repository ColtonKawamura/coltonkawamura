## Part A

Let's review the critter for electric fields.

1. They have no curl.
2. They are conservative.

Let's put these criteria to use.


$$
\begin{align*}
\nabla \times \mathbf{E} &= \nabla \times k[x y\hat{\bf x}+2y z\,\hat{\bf y}+3x z\,\hat{\bf z}] \\
&= \begin{vmatrix}
1 & 4 & 1 \\ 
0 & -2 & 1 \\ 
0 & 2 & 1
\end{vmatrix}
\end{align*}
$$
