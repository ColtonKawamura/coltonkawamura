## Part A

To find the potential, we need to find the electric field. Luckily, we found the electric field in Example 2.1 in the text.

Let's take a line integral from infinity to the point $$z$$ to find the potential, 

$$
\begin{align*}
V&= -\int_\infty^{z'} \mathbf{E} \cdot d\bf{l}\\
\end{align*}
$$
